About me

I grew up with the passion for technology
currently, I am working on some full stack projects
and studying on my free time.

Thanks for stopping by at my Portfolio Website. A little story about me: I was born in Italy. I achieved my qualification in Business Management. However, few years later I found myself working as a Chef - driven by the love for food and hospitality. I moved to London by the age of 23 to explore a big city. After three years, I felt like I needed more and moved to Australia.

Since I was a child, I always had the passion for Technology. My first computer was an Intel 486 running Windows 3.1. I remember spending days just to try and figure out how things worked. A couple of years back, I took the decision to gain some web development skills through an online course. Since then, I joined a Diploma of Website Development which will be finished by August this year.

I am currently working as a Territory Manager for a food distributor here in Brisbane, and open to freelancer work as a Web Developer.

I am planning to learn new libraries like React and languages like Python. In the future, I see myself improving my skills as a Website Developer and further my education.
