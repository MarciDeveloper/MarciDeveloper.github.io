About me

Thanks for stopping by at my Portfolio Website, a little story about me, I was born in Italy, there I achieved my qualification in Business Management, however few years later I found myself working as a Chef, driven by the love for food and hospitality. I moved to London by the age of 23 to explore a big city and grow as a professional and individual. After three years i felt like I needed more and moved to Australia as it was always a childhood dream of mine.

Since I was a child, I always had the passion for Technlogy, my first computer was a Intel 486 running Windows 3.1. I remember spending days just to try and figure out how things worked.
A couple of years back, I took the decision to learn some Development skills through an online course and that is how I started. Since then, I joined a Diploma of Website Development that should be finished by August.

I am currently working as a Territory Manager for a food distributor here in Brisbane, and open to freelancer work as a Web Developer.

In the future I see myself improving my skills as a Developer and continue my education.
I am planning to learn new libraries like React and languages like Python.
